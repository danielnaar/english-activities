Understand the core structure before you start typing.

Golden Rule: Keep the CTA clear = ONE ask per email. Always add a deadline and an owner.

Subject Lines That Work

Keep it under 60 characters. Put the action word first.

Action

  • › Approval needed by [Fri]
  • › Confirm your slot 10:30 or 14:00
  • › Docs for review attached

Update

  • › Project X - weekly update
  • › Status + next steps
  • › Summary of today's call

Relationship

  • › Great to connect at [event]
  • › Thanks for your help with [topic]
  • › Intro to [Name] re [topic]

  • [Option 1: Day, Time, Timezone]
  • [Option 2: Day, Time, Timezone]

[Your name]

Hi Team,

Quick update on [Project]:

  • Done: [Action completed]
  • Next: [Next step]

[Your name]

Apology

Subject: Apologies for [issue]

Hi [Name],

I apologize for the confusion regarding [issue]. I realize this caused a delay.

I have now [action taken to fix it]. To ensure this doesn't happen again, I will [preventive action].

Thank you for your patience.

Best,
[Your name]

Complaint

Subject: Issue with [Product/Service/Invoice #]

Dear [Name/Support Team],

I am writing to report an issue with [Product/Service]. On [Date], [describe the factual issue].

Because of this, [effect it had].

Could you please [expected action, e.g., refund/replace/investigate] by [Date]?

Regards,
[Your name]

Networking

Hi [Name],

It was great meeting you at [Event] yesterday. I really enjoyed our chat about [Topic].

I'd love to stay in touch and learn more about your work at [Company]. Let me know if you ever have 15 minutes for a virtual coffee.

Best,
[Your name]

Rules & Etiquette

DO

  • ✔ Clear subject with one action.
  • ✔ One topic per email.
  • ✔ Short paragraphs (2-3 lines).
  • ✔ Use dates and time zones: Thu 12 Dec, 10:30 am ET.
  • ✔ Name files clearly: invoice_2025-10-04.pdf.

DON'T

  • ✘ Walls of text or multiple asks.
  • ✘ ALL CAPS or too many exclamation marks.
  • ✘ Ambiguous words: soon, later, ASAP.
  • ✘ Hidden requests inside long threads.
  • ✘ Attachments with generic names (e.g. document1.pdf).

Etiquette, CC & Attachments

1

Response Time

Same day for internal emails, 24-48h for external clients.

2

CC vs BCC

CC is for visibility. Use BCC for large lists or to drop a person after an intro to spare their inbox.

3

Attachments

Call them out in the body: "2 files attached: contract.pdf and plan.docx."

4

Thread Discipline

When changing the topic completely, start a new thread with a new subject line.
