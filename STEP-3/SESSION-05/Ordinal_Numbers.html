Ordinal Numbers

Explore the rules and applications of ordinal numbers to express sequences, dates, and rankings accurately.

What are Ordinal Numbers?

Ordinal numbers show position or order: first, second, third...

Basic Ordinals (1 to 10)

1st first
2nd second
3rd third
4th fourth
5th fifth
6th sixth
7th seventh
8th eighth
9th ninth
10th tenth

Spelling Rules

  • •
    Add -th to most numbers.
    (11th = eleventh, 20th = twentieth)
  • •
    Exceptions: 1st = first, 2nd = second, 3rd = third, 12th = twelfth.
  • •
    For decades: 21st = twenty first, 32nd = thirty second, 43rd = forty third.

Main Uses

  • 1
    Dates:
    "Today is the 21st of March."
  • 2
    Competitions:
    "She finished in 2nd place."
  • 3
    Sequences:
    "This is the 3rd exercise."
