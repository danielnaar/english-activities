Likes & Dislikes

Expressing preferences accurately is a core social skill. Master the spectrum from passion to aversion with professional nuance.

The Preference Spectrum

Love

Expressions
adore be crazy about love
  • "I love jogging at night."
  • "She adores Italian food."
  • "He's crazy about football."

Like

Expressions
like enjoy be into
  • "I like playing golf."
  • "He really enjoys cooking."
  • "I'm into sci-fi movies."
-

Neutral

Expressions
don't mind it's OK
  • "I don't mind taking out the trash."
  • "It's all the same to me."

Dislike

Expressions
don't like not a fan not fond of
  • "I don't like waiting in line."
  • "She's not a big fan of spicy food."
  • "He's not very fond of doing homework."

Hate

Expressions
hate detest can't stand
  • "I hate bursting balloons."
  • "He can't stand traffic jams."
  • "She detests celery."
