EXECUTIVE GRAMMAR MODULE

Comparatives & Superlatives

Understanding how to compare things in English

1

Core Mechanics

Short Adjectives

Use -er / -est with short (one-syllable) adjectives.

small ➔ smaller ➔ smallest tall ➔ taller ➔ tallest

Long Adjectives

Use more / most with longer adjectives (2+ syllables).

more beautiful the most expensive

The Connector

Don't forget to use "than" right after comparatives.

"This car is faster than mine."
2

The Transformation Matrix

Adjective Comparative Superlative
small smaller (the) smallest
difficult more difficult (the) most difficult
unusual more unusual (the) most unusual
interesting more interesting (the) most interesting
many Quantifier more most (countable nouns)
little Quantifier less least
