Present Perfect Simple

Learn how and when to connect past events to the present moment. Master the structure and key expressions.

Main Uses

Past Actions (No Time)

- I've travelled to Canada twice.

States Continuing Now

- I have lived in Hamilton since 1990.

Past Actions with Results

- I'm tired. I've just finished studying.

- Look! Daniel has broken his leg.

Structure

SUBJECT
+
have / has
+
PAST
PARTICIPLE
  • She has eaten
  • He has worked
  • We have studied

Essential Tips

  • SINCE Starting point in time (since 2010).
  • FOR Duration of time (for 5 years).
  • EVER / NEVER
    To talk about life experiences. - Have you ever tried sushi? - I've never been to Paris.

Watch Out!

I have finished my homework yesterday.
I went to the cinema yesterday.

Rule of Thumb

If the result matters now → Present Perfect

If the exact time matters → Past Simple
