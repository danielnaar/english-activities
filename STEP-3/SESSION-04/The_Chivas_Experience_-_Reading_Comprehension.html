Chivas, ra, ra, ra!

I've never liked football. I don't know the rules, I can't name the players, and honestly, I've never cared. But somehow, for most of my life, people have connected me with one team: the Chivas Rayadas de Guadalajara.

It all started when I was a kid. Everyone kept asking, "Which team do you support?" I didn't have an answer. One day, watching Mexican TV, I heard the name Chivas Rayadas de Guadalajara. It sounded funny, so I decided to use it as my "team," just to stop the questions.

The opposite happened. Whenever I said "I'm with Chivas," people got curious. In Canada, that name sounded exotic, and nobody forgot it. Over the years, friends gave me Chivas gifts like caps, shirts, mugs, even scarves, as if I were actually a fan of the team.

The "joke" went so far that I eventually traveled to Mexico and attended a real Chivas match. The stadium was amazing, the fans were crazy, and the experience was unforgettable... I did it just to play along with everyone who decided I was a fan. Even though I still don't like football, I'm not even a fan of any team.
