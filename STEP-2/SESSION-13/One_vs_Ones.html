One / Ones

Replace nouns without repeating them. Master this quick technique for a more natural, fluid, and professional conversational style.

The Replacement Rule

1

Singular ➔ one

Use one to avoid repeating a singular countable noun.

✓
"Which coat is yours?" "The black one."
Transformations
book ➔ the red one phone ➔ the new one student ➔ the tall one
+

Plural ➔ ones

Use ones to avoid repeating a plural countable noun.

✓
"Which shoes do you like?" "The brown ones."
Transformations
apples ➔ the green ones cars ➔ the small ones friends ➔ the funny ones

Quick Note

Don't use one/ones after uncountable nouns.

✕ the milk one
✓ Just say: "the milk"
