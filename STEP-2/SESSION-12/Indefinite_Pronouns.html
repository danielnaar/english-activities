Compounds of SOME, ANY, NO, EVERY

SOME

People someone,
somebody
Things something
Places somewhere

Meaning & when

Yes vibe. We expect the answer to be yes or we are offering something.

affirmative offers & requests unspecified
Polite offer: "Would you like something to drink?"

ANY

People anyone,
anybody
Things anything
Places anywhere

Meaning & when

In questions and negatives. In affirmative, means no limit or it does not matter which.

questions negatives it does not matter which
"Pick any seat. I do not mind."

NO

People no one,
nobody
Things nothing
Places nowhere

Meaning & when

Makes a negative idea by itself. Use with an affirmative verb. Do not add another not.

negative idea verb is affirmative avoid double negatives
! "I have no time."
Not: I do not have no time.

EVERY

People everyone,
everybody
Things everything
Places everywhere

Meaning & when

Means all. Usually takes a singular verb: Everyone is, Everything looks.

all 100 percent singular verb
Good news: "everyone passed."

Golden patterns

•Some for offers and friendly requests:

"Could you bring something to drink?"

•Any for questions and negatives:

"Do you have any questions? We do not have any time."

•No gives a negative alone:

"There is no coffee left."

•Every means all:

"Everyone is invited."

Look out for these

•No double negatives:

✓ I do not know anyone here.
✕ Not: I do not know no one.

•No one is two words. Nobody is one word.

Both are fine.

•Everyone and everybody take a singular verb:

Everyone is ready.
