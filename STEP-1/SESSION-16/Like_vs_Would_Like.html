Like vs Would like

Understand the difference between general preferences and polite wishes.

LIKE

We use like to talk about things we enjoy in general.

  • • I like pizza.
    → General preference
  • • She likes playing tennis.
    → Hobby or habit
  • • They like traveling.
    → Something they enjoy anytime

Think:

"What makes me happy usually?"

WOULD LIKE

We use would like to talk about something we want now, or in a polite way.

  • • I would like a glass of water.
    → A wish right now
  • • Would you like some tea?
    → Polite offer
  • • We'd like to visit Canada next year.
    → A plan or desire

Think:

"What do I want at this moment?, or politely in the future?"
