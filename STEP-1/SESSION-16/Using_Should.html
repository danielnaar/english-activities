Using Should

Master the use of "should" for giving advice, recommendations, and personal opinions.

Core Applications

Understand the different contexts for using this modal verb.

1

Giving Advice

Use should to suggest what is best or helpful.

  • • You should speak to him about it.
  • • He should see a doctor.
  • • We should ask a lawyer.
2

Giving Opinions /
Recommendations

It shows what you think is the right or smart choice.

  • • He should resign now.
  • • We should invest more in Asia.
  • • They should do something about this terrible train service.
3

Expressing Personal
Opinions

Should is weaker and more personal than must or have to. It is often used after I think...

  • • I think they should replace him.
  • • I don't think they should keep the contract.
  • • Do you think we should tell her?

4) Quick Recap

Advice

Good suggestion.

→ "You should eat healthier".

Opinion

Personal view.

→ "I think we should wait".

Weaker than must

Not an obligation, just guidance.

"Think of should as a soft, polite push in the right direction."
