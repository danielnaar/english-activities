Regular and Irregular Verbs

Mastering the spelling patterns for the Simple Past tense.

Regular Verbs

Verb ending in... How to make simple past Examples
e Add -D
live → lived date → dated
consonant + y Change Y to I, then add -ED
try → tried
Vowel + consonant
(but not w or y) 		Double the consonant, then add -ED
tap → tapped commit → committed
Anything else
(including w) 		Add -ED
boil → boiled fill → filled hand → handed show → showed
