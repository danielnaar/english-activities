How to Say Phone Numbers
in English

Mastering the correct rhythm and vocabulary for phone numbers is essential for professional, clear, and executive communication.

Basic Digits & Zero

We usually say phone numbers in single digits:

437-2963

four three seven, two nine six three

When "0" is in a number, it's pronounced "oh":

237-0980

two three seven, oh nine eight oh

Repeated & Toll-Free

Repeated digits can use "double":

773-4455

double seven three, double four double five

*Optional but common in the UK and spoken English

Toll-Free Numbers (Grouped):

1-800-555-1212

one, eight hundred, five five five, one two one two

Practice! Say these out loud:

• 604-801-0044 → six oh four, eight oh one, double oh double four
• 1-888-333-9900 → one, eight eight eight, triple three, nine nine oh oh
• 514-777-3456 → five one four, triple seven, three four five six

Tips:

  • • Say numbers one by one unless grouped (like "eight hundred").
  • • Use "oh" instead of "zero" when reading phone numbers.
  • • Optional: Use "double" for repeated digits to sound more fluent. Pause briefly at dashes (-) or between groups.
