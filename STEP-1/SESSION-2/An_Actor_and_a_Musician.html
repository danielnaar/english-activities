PREMIUM ENGLISH

An Actor and a Musician

Executive Dialogue Practice

Scene I

Actor

"Hello! I see a guitar. What is your job?"

Musician

"I am a musician. Why do you have a script?"

Scene II

Actor

"I am an actor. I have an audition today."

Musician

"That is interesting! Where do you work as an actor?"

Scene III

Actor

"I work in a theater. Who is in your band?"

Musician

"I have a drummer and a singer. When is your audition?"

Scene IV

Actor

"It is in an hour. How do you write a song?"

Musician

"I play an acoustic guitar and write a melody."

Scene V

Actor

"That is a great talent. I want a role in a music video!"

Musician

"I actually need an actor for a new video!"

Scene VI

Actor

"What is the video about? Is it an acoustic song?"

Musician

"Yes, it is an acoustic song in a cafe. Let's make an agreement!"

Conclusion

Resolution

"Perfect! Being an actor or a musician is a wonderful adventure."
