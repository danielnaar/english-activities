-teen vs. -ty Numbers

In English, numbers from 13 to 19 usually have the stress on the final syllable:
the "-teen".

Numbers like 30, 40, 50... have the stress at the beginning: THIRty, FORTY, FIFty, etc.

Teen Numbers

  • thirTEEN
  • fifTEEN
  • sixTEEN
  • nineTEEN

-ty Numbers

  • THIRty
  • FIFty
  • SIXty
  • NINEty

Helpful Tip:

  • • Say teen numbers clearly, with a long stressed "-teen" (like "thirteen").
  • • Say -ty numbers with strong stress at the start (like "thirty").
  • • Be careful! Saying the wrong stress may confuse "14" and "40", "16" and "60".
