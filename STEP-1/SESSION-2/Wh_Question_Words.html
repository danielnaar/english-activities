WH-Question Words

Master the essential words for asking questions in English.

Who

People

"Who is your teacher?"

What

Things / Info

"What is your name?"

When

Time

"When is your birthday?"

Where

Place

"Where do you live?"

Why

Reason

"Why are you late?"

Which

Choice / Options

"Which color do you prefer?"

How

Manner / Way

"How do you cook rice?"

Whose

Possession

"Whose bag is this?"

Strategic Tips

  • ■
    Who is always about a person: Who is that man?
  • ■
    What is general, Which is for limited options: What is your job? Which job do you want?
  • ■
    Why is followed by a reason with because.
  • ■
    How can combine with adjectives: How old, How tall, How far, How long...

Common Mistakes

✕ What do you born?
✓ When were you born?
✕ Why you are sad?
✓ Why are you sad?
✕ Who is this bag?
✓ Whose bag is this?
✕ What color do you prefer?
✓ Which color do you prefer?
