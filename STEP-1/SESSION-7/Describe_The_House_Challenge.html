PREMIUM ENGLISH

Describe the House

Look at the picture carefully. Use There is / There are combined with Prepositions of Place to describe exact locations.

Examples

  • • There is a cat in the basement.
  • • There are two lamps in the bedroom.
  • • There is a table in the dining room.

Practice Time!

Look at the dollhouse carefully. In your notebook or speaking out loud, create at least 5 to 10 sentences describing what you see and exactly where it is.

Checklist for Success:

  • ✓ Use There is for singular items.
  • ✓ Use There are for plural items.
  • ✓
    Use Prepositions of Place to give details.

    Basic: in, on, at

    Complex: under, next to, between, above
