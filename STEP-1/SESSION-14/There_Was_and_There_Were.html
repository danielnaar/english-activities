PREMIUM ENGLISH • PAST TENSE

There was / There were

To talk about existence in the past, we simply change the verb to be from present to past.

Core Rule

was

Use for

Singular or Uncountable Nouns.

(one, much, water, furniture)

were

Use for

Plural Nouns.

(two+, many, chairs, people)

Tense Change

There is one chair. → There was one chair.
There are many chairs. → There were many chairs.
There is water. → There was water.
! Tip: After "there", look at the noun! One/Water = was. Two/Many = were.
