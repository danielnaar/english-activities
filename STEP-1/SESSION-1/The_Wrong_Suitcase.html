PREMIUM ENGLISH

The Wrong Suitcase

Executive Dialogue Practice

Scene I

Speaker A

"Excuse me, is this your suitcase?"

Speaker B

"No, it isn't. My suitcase is blue."

Scene II

Speaker A

"Oh, I see. Is that man your husband?"

Speaker B

"Yes, he is. His name is Mark."

Scene III

Speaker A

"Look! He has a blue suitcase in his hand."

Speaker B

"Wait, that is not our suitcase."

Scene IV

Speaker A

"Are you sure? It looks very similar."

Speaker B

"Yes, our suitcase has a red ribbon."

Scene V

Speaker A

"Maybe those people over there have your luggage."

Speaker B

"You mean the couple? What is their nationality?"

Scene VI

Speaker A

"I think they are French. Their bags are over there."

Speaker B

"Excuse me! Is that your red ribbon on that bag?"

Conclusion

French Couple

"Yes, it is! Thank you for your help."

Speaker B

"You're welcome. Have a nice flight!"
