Breakfast Around the World 🍳

Breakfast is more than just the first meal of the day, it's a window into culture. Let's explore three surprising morning traditions from around the globe.

🇸🇪 Sweden

In Sweden, breakfast is clean, cold, and simple. A slice of crispbread topped with cheese, cucumber or cold meats is common. Yogurt with muesli or fruit and a strong cup of black coffee ☕ are part of the routine. Many people also enjoy a boiled egg 🥚 with salt. It's nutritious, minimal, and beautifully arranged.

🇨🇴 Colombia

In the Andean region of Colombia, a popular breakfast is caldo de costilla 🍲, a beef rib broth with potato, coriander, and garlic. It's typically served steaming hot, often before sunrise. People pair it with arepas, coffee, and sometimes even hot chocolate 🍫 with cheese. It's hearty, strong, and perfect for early workers and long days.

🇲🇦 Morocco

A Moroccan breakfast is fragrant and full of textures. You might find fresh flatbreads 🫓 like msmen or baghrir served with honey and butter. Black olives, hard-boiled eggs, mint tea 🫖, and goat cheese often complete the meal. It's a slow and social experience, usually shared with family.
